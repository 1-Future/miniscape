Tutorial Island Launch

7 March 2026

Tutorial Island is live in MiniScape — a full 26-step recreation built entirely from data captured during a real OSRS playthrough. No guessing, no wiki scraping. Every NPC position, every item given, every XP drop comes from actual game events.

The Approach: Data-Driven Authenticity

We created a new OSRS account and played through Tutorial Island with our Combat Debug Plugin running. The plugin logged 1,474 lines of raw game data — every NPC interaction, every inventory change, every animation ID.

From that data, we extracted:

  • 10 guide NPCs with exact definition IDs and positions
  • 4 combat NPCs (giant rats and chickens) with HP and stats
  • 26 progression steps from Gielinor Guide to the final teleport
  • 18 items in the completion inventory
  • Every skill XP value and animation ID

Step Progression

Tutorial Island follows a strict linear progression. Each step is either a talk step (dialogue with an NPC, sometimes receiving items) or an action step (fishing, woodcutting, combat, etc.).

Survival Skills (Steps 2–5)

The Survival Expert teaches fishing, woodcutting, firemaking, and cooking. Players catch 2 shrimps, chop 2 trees, light 2 fires, and cook 2 shrimps — earning their first XP in four different skills.

Cooking (Steps 6–7)

The Master Chef provides flour and water. Players combine them into bread dough and cook it on the range — 40 Cooking XP for their first bread.

Mining & Smithing (Steps 10–13)

Underground, the Mining Instructor guides players through mining tin and copper, smelting a bronze bar at the furnace, and smithing a bronze dagger on the anvil.

Combat Triangle (Steps 14–17)

The Combat Instructor covers all three styles:

  • Melee — Bronze sword + wooden shield vs Giant rat (anim 386)
  • Ranged — Shortbow + bronze arrows vs Giant rat (anim 426)

Magic (Steps 23–24)

The Magic Instructor provides air and mind runes. Players cast Wind Strike (anim 711) on a chicken for their first magic XP.

Completion

After all 26 steps, the Magic Instructor teleports the player to Lumbridge (3222, 3218) with a standardized starter inventory of 18 items — matching exactly what a real OSRS player receives.

Every value in tutorial-island.js was captured from a real game session. When OSRS gives you 25 Woodcutting XP for chopping a tree on Tutorial Island, MiniScape gives you 25 Woodcutting XP.
